My top 3 albums of all time

Lupe Fiasco's Food & Liquor

Story of a kid growing up on the westside of chicago. Classic Hip Hop album with a nerdy twist.

The Story So Far - Under Soil & Dirt

Angsty pop punk describing the stages of a breakup

Turnover - Peripheral Vision

Dream Pop album going over drug abuse and other mental health issues
